How to use this tool

This generator creates a customized NeuGroup proposal and outreach email for any PE sponsor. Follow the five steps in order.

1 Sponsor details

Enter the firm name, your contact, their title and email, the functional group you're proposing, and today's date.

2 Tier 1 — Single meeting

Set member count, meeting format (in-person or virtual), and whether to include a research project. Price builds automatically.

3 Tier 2 — Annual program

Set members, number of in-person and virtual sessions per year, and research. Toggle off either tier to present only one option.

4 Additional context

Add sponsor background — how you know them, their priorities, timing. Add tone notes for the email (e.g. "keep it short, we met at a conference").

5 Generate & export

Click Generate proposal, then Generate email. Review both previews. Click Download .docx for the formatted proposal and Download email for the outreach copy.

💡 Billing note: The proposal includes a callout that billing is flexible — sponsors can recharge fees to portcos or NeuGroup can invoice directly.

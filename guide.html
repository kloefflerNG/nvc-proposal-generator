How to use the Proposal Generator
1
Choose your proposal type
At the top of the page, select one of three types: NeuGroup for Value Creation (PE sponsor peer group), Sponsored Solution Session (virtual SSS), or In-Person Session (event partnership). The form updates instantly.
2
Fill in sponsor and rep details
Enter the sponsor firm name and your own NeuGroup rep name, title, and email. These appear in the proposal header and closing footer.
3
Configure pricing
Enter meeting counts, member numbers, and research options. The pricing summary updates in real time — no manual calculation needed. For SSS, add each session separately. For In-Person, fill in the event details and edit the pre-filled entitlement rows.
4
Add context (this matters)
The AI uses your context notes to write the proposal narrative. The more specific you are, the better the output. Include: how you met them, their website or focus area, what they care about, timing, and any relevant background. A sentence or two is enough — but specifics beat generics every time.
5
Generate and download
Click Generate proposal, then Generate email. Review both in the preview. When ready, click Download .docx for the branded proposal and Download email for the outreach copy. Open the .docx in Word — it's ready to review and send.
Proposal types — what each one covers
NVC NeuGroup for Value Creation — PE Sponsor Peer Group

For PE sponsors who want to run a peer group for CFOs or other functional leaders across their portfolio companies. Pricing is built from components — in-person meetings ($42,500 each), virtual sessions ($20,000 each), and an optional research project ($20,000).

The proposal presents two tiers (single meeting vs. annual program) and explains the upgrade credit structure. The billing flexibility callout is automatically included.

Sponsor firm name Functional group (CFO, Treasurer, etc.) Member count In-person and virtual session counts Research project toggle Member seat fee Your NeuGroup rep info Sponsor background (context)
SSS Sponsored Solution Session

For sponsors buying access to a specific NeuGroup peer group via a virtual session. Default price is $20,000 per session. Add multiple sessions for multi-option proposals — each gets its own "Option N" table in the proposal.

Each session has editable "You receive" and "Timing" fields that flow directly into the proposal table. The AI writes a tailored intro paragraph based on your context notes.

Sponsor name and contact Session name / peer group Format (virtual 60 or 90 min) Audience description Content focus Price per session You receive (pre-filled, editable) Timing (pre-filled, editable)
IPS In-Person Session — Event Partnership

For partners sponsoring a NeuGroup in-person event. The proposal includes an Event Details Summary table and a Partner Access and Entitlements table — both pulled directly from your inputs, no extra formatting needed.

Entitlement rows are pre-filled with the standard set (Networking Dinner Access, Featured Session, Guest Invitations, etc.) but every row is editable. You can remove rows that don't apply or add new ones.

Partner name and contact Event / peer group name Season and event dates Location and format Partnership fee Payment terms Term end date Entitlement rows (editable)
How to use the Pitch Deck Generator
1
Fill in partner and rep details
Enter the partner company name, your NeuGroup rep name, title, and email. These appear in the deck header and the closing "Let's Talk" slide. If you used the Proposal Generator first, these fields may pre-fill automatically.
2
New or repeat sponsor?
Toggle New sponsor or Repeat sponsor. If repeat, a history section appears — add each past sponsorship with the period (e.g. H1 2024), session name, dollar amount, and NeuGroup contact. This populates a full sponsorship timeline slide in the deck.
3
Edit the collaboration options
The three partnership options on slide 6 are pre-filled with standard language. Edit the titles and descriptions to match what you're actually pitching to this specific partner.
4
Add context and levers
Write a few sentences about why this partner and NeuGroup are a natural fit. List their key value levers or focus areas (e.g. "working capital, M&A integration, digital transformation"). The AI uses both to write the custom narrative slides.
5
Generate and download
Click Generate deck copy to preview the AI-written slides. Review the content in the preview box. When ready, click Download .pptx — the deck opens in PowerPoint, fully formatted and ready to present or refine.
PPTX What's in the pitch deck

💡 Tips for better output
→ The context field is the biggest lever for quality. A sentence about how you met them or what they care about makes the AI copy noticeably more specific and relevant.
→ If you don't love the AI output, click Regenerate — it writes fresh copy each time. Tweak the context notes first for best results.
→ The Proposal Generator and Pitch Deck Generator share data in the same browser session. Fill in the proposal first and the pitch deck will pre-fill the matching fields automatically.
→ All downloaded files open in Word or PowerPoint and are fully editable — treat them as a strong first draft, not a final product.
→ The password is shared across the team. If it ever needs to change, contact whoever manages the GitHub repo.
